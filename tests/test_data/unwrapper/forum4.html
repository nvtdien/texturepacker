Welcome to Forgotten Lands!

I've been developing this texture packs for more than 8 months (since Alpha), and finally it can see the light.
My goal was to create a photo-realistic texture pack that runs at standard 16x16 resolution, i.e. does not require any mods. Well, people say it's not quite photo-realistic, but it does give an illusion of higher resolution textures It is brightly coloured, smooth and biome-oriented: you can find autumn forests and even grow a frozen park in tundra.

Webpage and download: fl.xrbpowered.com

Gallery: fl.xrbpowered.com/gallery.html

If you have an older version cached in your browser, click Reload or press F5

Current version: 1.4, 28 September 2011
Compatible with Minecraft Beta update 1.9

What's new:
  • Basic 1.9 Pre-release compatibility: terrain and items updated; mobs skins are still default.
  • Biomes updated for new terrain generation algorithm.
  • Pig snouts.
  • Stone bricks redone again.
